Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Cheese
  • Flat Pasta Sheet
  • Red Sauce
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Place flat pasta sheet in pan
  2. Pour in red sauce
  3. Pour in browned ground beef
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Bake at 425(f) for 2.5 hours
